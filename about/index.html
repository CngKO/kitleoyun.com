OUR MISSION

To bring joy to millions

We are a brand new
game development studio


KitleOyun is a game development studio that was founded in 2018. We have
started off with design and development of several strategy games targeted
for mobile devices. We are focusing on free-to-play games for strategy
lovers out there. Our first game, Tribes of Isoterra will initially be
released on Google Play platform in summer 2019.

Our culture


At KitleOyun, we have a very simple vision for achieving great results - we think
the output should come on top of the hours you put in. This vision is
what initially motivated us and what fuels us every day. We are
always hungry for knowledge and ready to take up new challenges
believing that the end result will always be higher quality games. A
sense of personal responsibility and commitment to our mission are
the driving force while we strive to bring joy to those who play our games.
We love what we do and hope that everyone else loves it too!
